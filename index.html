About

About Sansad Q&A

Empowering Citizens Through Transparency

Sansad Q&A is a platform dedicated to making Indian Parliament's question-and-answer sessions accessible to every citizen. We aggregate official data from Parliament records, the Press Information Bureau (PIB), and other government sources to present verified questions and answers in an easy-to-understand format.

Every question you see here was officially raised by a Member of Parliament during a parliamentary session. The answers are provided by the respective government ministries. We ensure factual accuracy by cross-referencing with official Lok Sabha and Rajya Sabha records.

Data Sources

Our data is sourced from official Indian Government channels:

  • ● Lok Sabha Official Website (loksabha.nic.in)
  • ● Rajya Sabha Official Website (rajyasabha.nic.in)
  • ● Press Information Bureau (pib.gov.in)
  • ● National Informatics Centre (NIC)
